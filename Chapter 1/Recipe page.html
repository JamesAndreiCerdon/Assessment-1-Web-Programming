Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a classic Indian dish known for its rich and creamy tomato-based sauce. It's a must-try for curry lovers!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method:

  1. In a large bowl, marinate the chicken pieces in yogurt, garlic, ginger, and spices. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until translucent.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Stir in tomato puree and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Reduce heat, add heavy cream, and simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot with naan or rice.
